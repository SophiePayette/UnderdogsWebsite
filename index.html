BUILD YOUR LEGACY

or just try your best, we don't judge.

Heroes of Tomorrow is a management and turn-based combat game where you lead a company of flawed superheroes. Plan missions, balance profits, and manage public perception to keep your organization afloat!

STRATEGY

Turn-based tactical combat that rewards brainpower over button mashing. Plan your strike, save the day!

CUSTOMIZE

Build your ultimate hero with their own personality, powers, and questionable fashion choices.

MORAL SYSTEM

Your heroes are human too (in their own way)… and nothing is more human than flaws. Manage their moods wisely, or be ready to face the consequences.
